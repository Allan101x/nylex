Who We Are

Engineering Excellence Since 2011

Nylex International (PVT) Ltd is a Zimbabwean construction and civil works company based in Gletwyn, Harare. We deliver end-to-end solutions in building construction, civil engineering works, renovations & fit-outs, and facilities maintenance.

Our delivery model blends disciplined project management, rigorous quality control, and responsible procurement to achieve on-time, on-budget outcomes aligned to client objectives.

With multi-disciplinary experience across residential, commercial and light industrial projects, we operate a safety-first culture and transparent communication—supported by weekly dashboards, photo evidence and structured reporting.

Commitment to Excellence

Health, Safety & Quality

Health, Safety & Environment

Nylex operates a zero-harm philosophy aligned to Zimbabwean legislation. Every task has a Job Hazard Analysis; PPE is enforced; waste is segregated with licensed handlers.

Quality Assurance

Our QA system embeds inspection & test plans (ITPs), calibrated tools, documented hold-points and approvals. Non-conformances are recorded and investigated.

Governance & Ethics

We adhere to sound corporate governance: fair competition, anti-corruption compliance, and transparent subcontractor engagement.
